To prepare pasta:

1. Boil 2 liters of water
2. Add 10 gramms of salt to water
3. Add 100 gramms of spaghetti into boiling water
4. Mix pasta slightly for at least 2 minutes
5. Lower temperature a bit but keep water boiling
6. Wait about 10 minutes
7. Remove water
8. Add some butter

Pasta is ready!
